# All-Staff Meeting Facilitator Guide

## Your Role as Meeting Facilitator

As a rotating facilitator, you are responsible for leading and coordinating the monthly All-Staff meeting. This guide outlines the new agenda format and your specific responsibilities.

---

## New All-Staff Meeting Agenda

| Segment | Duration | Owner |
|---------|----------|-------|
| **Ecosystem Topic** | 5 minutes | Facilitator (your choice) |
| **Major Internal Topic/Update** | 10-15 minutes | Facilitator + Exec Team |
| **Internal TED Talk / Org Highlights** | 10-15 minutes | Facilitator + Participants |
| **Client Story** | 5 minutes | Programs or Lending |
| **Announcements** | 5 minutes | All Departments (1-2 each) |
| **Shoutouts** | 5 minutes | All Departments (1-2 each) |

**Total Meeting Time:** ~45-50 minutes

---

## Facilitator Responsibilities

### 2+ Weeks Before the Meeting

1. **Ecosystem Topic (5 min)**
   - Select a relevant industry or ecosystem topic to share
   - Prepare a brief overview or discussion point
   - Examples: policy updates, industry trends, community news

2. **Major Internal Topic/Update (10-15 min)**
   - Reach out to the Executive Team to determine the main topic
   - Coordinate with the appropriate presenter(s)
   - Confirm content and any presentation materials needed

3. **Internal TED Talk / Org Highlights (10-15 min)**
   - Choose a topic and identify presenter(s)
   - Confirm participation and coordinate content

   **Suggested Topics & Presenters:**
   | Topic | Potential Presenters |
   |-------|---------------------|
   | AAC PR Strategy | Messay, Kaelyn, Megan |
   | Fusion Festival | Messay, Jess |
   | Hive Transition | Messay, Jess |
   | Sustainable DC | Geneva, Megan |

4. **Client Story (5 min)**
   - Contact Programs or Lending team
   - Request a client story presentation
   - Confirm presenter and timing

### 1 Week Before the Meeting

5. **Collect Announcements & Shoutouts**
   - Send reminder to all departments
   - Request 1-2 announcements per department
   - Request 1-2 shoutouts per department
   - Compile submissions

6. **Finalize Agenda**
   - Confirm all presenters
   - Create/review slide deck if applicable
   - Share final agenda with Lovesha Graham

### Day of Meeting

7. **Facilitate the Meeting**
   - Open and welcome attendees
   - Introduce each segment and presenter
   - Keep time and transitions smooth
   - Close with any final notes
