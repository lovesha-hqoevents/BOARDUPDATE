## Invite Body

All-Staff Monthly Meeting

Join us for our monthly All-Staff meeting. Each month, a rotating director facilitator leads the session.

**Agenda:**
1. Welcome & Ecosystem Topic (5 min)
2. Major Internal Update (10–15 min)
3. Internal TED Talk / Org Highlights (10–15 min)
4. Client Story (5 min)
5. Announcements (5 min)
6. Shoutouts (5 min)
7. Closing

Have announcements or shoutouts to share? Please submit them to Lovesha Graham at least 3 days before the meeting.

Questions about the meeting? Contact Lovesha Graham.
